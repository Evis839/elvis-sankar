What is a graphic designer?

A graphic designer is a professional who creates visual content using a computer in order to communicate messages in a clear, attractive, and creative way.

what is a DIgital Content Creator?

A digital content creator is a creative person who builds and shares engaging content online to connect with an audience.

what is a web developer?

A web developer is a person who builds and maintains websites or web applications. They use coding languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and others to create everything from simple web pages to complex online platforms.
